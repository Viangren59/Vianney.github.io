About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary engineer with a passion for prototyping and product design. I love blending mechanical systems with smart electronics and have developed everything from data center cooling tech to embedded sensor systems. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and precision.

Projects

A waterproof connector that collects power data and transmits it via BLE. Designed with custom PCBs and a glass-filled nylon enclosure.

Diagnosed and fixed a glitchy e-bike battery with intermittent motor failures. Developed a better connection and improved lifespan.

4. Solar Carport

Designed and prototyped a solar carport for EVs. Combines power electronics, user safety, and rapid installation structure.
